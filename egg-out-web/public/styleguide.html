Typography

One self-hosted family does everything: Capo (Light 300, Medium 500, Bold 700) — loaded via next/font/local from src/fonts/*.otf. The look is Capo Light at huge display sizes, tight tracking, a Farmer-orange period as punctuation. Eyebrows are Medium, uppercase, widely tracked.

Optical flush (signature detail)

.headline-flush · -mid · -open

A flush-left display glyph carries a left side-bearing, so its ink sits inward of the kicker/paragraph edge. A negative text-indent (em-based, bucketed by letter shape) pulls it back so the eye reads one clean left edge. The careful bit that makes the type feel set, not placed.

Dashed orange = corrected edge; grey = raw side-bearing.

Foundations

Motion

Motion is the personality. The headline acts are the 3D type-room intro and the scroll-built sandwich; underneath, a runny-egg cursor, an infinite marquee, soft scroll reveals, and a slow-spinning badge keep the page alive. Premium ease-out cubic-bezier(.22,1,.36,1) is the workhorse curve.

Kinetic type-room intro (signature)

KineticIntro.tsx

On load, "EGG & OUT" holds center on clean Farmer orange, then a one-point-perspective room of tiled wordmark text spirals in (dolly + spin) and flies through, handing off to the faint background pattern. Plays once, ~5s, then unmounts; scroll is locked underneath. Ref: 333southwabash.com "The Red".

Scroll-built sandwich (signature)

SandwichAssembly.tsx

A 360vh sticky stage: as you scroll, ingredient layers fly in from the right and stack — base, egg, lettuce, sauce, tomato — then the brioche top drops from above and the stack settles with a gentle scale & steam. The step list on the left lights Farmer as each layer lands. Ref: cravburgers.shop.
